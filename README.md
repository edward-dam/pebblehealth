### description
<br />

Health focused watchface, includes:
 - day of the week
 - sleep duration
 - battery percentage
 - time & date
 - current heart rate
 - current steps total
 - bluetooth symbol
<br />

Options:
 - choose between 24 hours or 12 hours
 - choose day/month date order
